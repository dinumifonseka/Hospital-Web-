Welcome!

Care Compass Hospitals is the most accredited hospital in the Sri Lankan healthcare sector. Since 2002, we have revolutionized the healthcare industry through infrastructure development and advanced services, delivering healthcare on par with global medical standards.

Whether you're visiting for routine care or specialized treatment, we are here to support you every step of the way. Your health journey starts here—trusted care, close to home.

Emergency Services

The Emergency Department at Care Compass hospital provides 24/7 urgent care for a wide range of medical emergencies. Our skilled team is equipped with advanced technology to deliver fast, effective treatment, prioritizing your health and safety in critical moments.

Outpatient Services

Care Compass hospital's Outpatient Services provide personalized care for routine check-ups, chronic conditions, and preventive treatments, ensuring a seamless and compassionate experience for all patients.

Laboratory Services

Care Compass hospital's Laboratory Services provide accurate, timely diagnostic testing, including blood work and imaging, to support diagnosis and treatment, ensuring efficient and reliable results.

Pharmacy Services

Care Compass hospital's Pharmacy Services provide safe, effective medication management. Our team of pharmacists offers expert advice, ensures proper dosage, and helps with prescription fulfillment to support your treatment and recovery.

Specialized Care

Care Compass hospital offers specialized care across various medical fields, with expert teams dedicated to providing advanced treatments for complex conditions. Our personalized approach ensures the highest level of care tailored to your needs.

Preventive and Wellness Services

Care Compass hospital provides Preventive and Wellness Services, including screenings, vaccinations, and personalized plans, to promote health and prevent illness.

Support Services

Care Compass hospital provides Support Services, including counseling and patient advocacy, to assist patients and families throughout their healthcare journey.
