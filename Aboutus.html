About Care Compass Hospitals

Care Compass Hospitals is the most accredited hospital in the Sri Lankan healthcare sector. Since 2002, we have revolutionized the healthcare industry through infrastructure development and advanced services, delivering healthcare on par with global medical standards.

Our mission is to provide compassionate care and advanced treatment to ensure the well-being of our patients. We are committed to excellence in healthcare and strive to be the trusted healthcare provider for our community.

Our Experience

Over the past 20 years, Care Compass Hospitals has grown to become a leader in the healthcare industry. Our team of experienced professionals has provided exceptional care to thousands of patients, earning a reputation for excellence and innovation.

We have continuously expanded our services and facilities to meet the evolving needs of our community. Our commitment to quality and patient satisfaction has been recognized through numerous awards and accreditations.




Our Qualifications

Our medical staff is comprised of highly qualified professionals with extensive training and experience in their respective fields. We are proud to have a team of board-certified doctors, nurses, and specialists who are dedicated to providing the highest standard of care.

Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and facilities, ensuring that our patients receive the best possible treatment. We are committed to continuous education and professional development to stay at the forefront of medical advancements.



Additional Information

At Care Compass Hospitals, we offer a wide range of medical services, including emergency care, specialized treatments, and wellness programs. Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced medical professionals ensure that you receive the highest quality care.

We are dedicated to continuous improvement and innovation in healthcare. Our team is constantly working to enhance our services and provide the best possible outcomes for our patients.
